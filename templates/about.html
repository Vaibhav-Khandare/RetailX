Problem Statement

Traditional inventory systems depend on manual record keeping, leading to stock mismatch, billing errors, and inefficient management.

Retail businesses face challenges such as inaccurate stock tracking, delayed billing, and lack of real-time inventory insights. RetailX solves these with centralized, automated, user-friendly management.

Project Objectives

  • Automate inventory and billing processes
  • Reduce human errors in stock management
  • Provide real-time inventory updates
  • Improve efficiency of retail operations
  • Support decision-making using analytics
