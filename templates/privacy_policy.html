PRIVACY POLICY

Your data security and privacy are our top priorities at RetailX

Our Commitment

RetailX is a smart inventory and billing system developed to help retailers manage inventory, billing, and business operations efficiently. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your business data.

Information Usage

RetailX only uses system-related data such as:

  • Product details and inventory records
  • Billing information and transaction history
  • User roles and access permissions
  • Business operational data

We only collect data necessary for the core functionality of the inventory management system.

Data Security

All data is securely stored and accessed only by authorized users. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure user authentication
  • Regular security audits
  • Role-based access control

Data Sharing

RetailX does not share user or business data with third parties. Your data remains confidential and is used solely for providing you with our inventory management services.

Data Retention

We retain your business data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting our team.
